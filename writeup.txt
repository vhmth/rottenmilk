We go through things in a priority-wise manner. Our main loop acts as a base
for all of the events we want to run in order. We have global variables that
tell us whether there's a ball to kick or a suduko puzzle to solve. These globals
allow us to update them within the interrupt handlers so that we don't disturb
the flow of the main loop.
